Bill Tracker

Save the links to each bill you pay monthly. Then, when it is time to pay the bills, they are all here ready for you!

  1. Navigate to the tab of the bill you would like to save
  2. Give the bill a name in the text box
  3. Hit "Save Current Tab"
  4. Repeat until all of your bills are saved below
  5. When it is time to pay the bills, open this extension and access each bill by clicking the links below
  6. Right now, the only way to delete items is to clear the list and start over. So proceed carefully 😄
